Coaching Staff

Josh Solk

Head Coach & Skill Development

With a lifelong love for basketball, I founded the Concrete Jungle Basketball Program. I started my journey as a player at Maple Ridge Secondary School and made my way to Columbia Bible College to play post-secondary, where I earned my stripes as a starting guard, including a standout performance of 25pts and 7 3's. Facing NCAA teams like SFU and WWU, I honed my skills. Now, having transitioned from player to coach, I'm eager to impart the wisdom I learned from top trainers and college teammates now turned pros. I'm committed to nurturing Maple Ridge's young talents, offering them a path to flourish in the game I cherish.

Cleo Kongolo

Assistant Coach & Athletic Development

Sports molded my identity from a young age. Despite lacking formal training, my natural talent stood out as I competed in youth soccer, as well as varsity football. Basketball, however, grew to become my true passion. I dedicated countless hours perfecting skills, excelling in defense and relentless pursuit. I focused strongly on my strength training to improve my athletic ability. Through Concrete Jungle Basketball, I aspire to inspire and empower the next generation with my expertise, fueled by an unyielding determination to succeed.
